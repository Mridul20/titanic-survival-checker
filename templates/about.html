Approach behind the model...
This is a simple calssification problem of whether a person will survive or not.We have quite a few algorithm for such binary classification problem.

  • Support Vector Machines
  • KNN or K-Nearest Neighbors
  • Random Forest
  • Gaussian Naive Bayes and many more...
After training the dataset with each model and using differnt metrics to evaluate like k-fold cross validation and F1 score I came to a conclusion that the Random Forest Classifier gives the highest accuracy.
It gives an accuracy of 94.52%
